About Me

I am a Research Scientist at the A*STAR Singapore Institute for Clinical Sciences (SICS), where my work is dedicated to advancing the field of mental health through research focused on cognition, mood, and sleep. I specialize in designing and implementing clinical studies and developing non-pharmacological, cost-effective interventions to improve mental health for a broad population. I am deeply committed to Open Science and conducting research with the highest level of rigor.
